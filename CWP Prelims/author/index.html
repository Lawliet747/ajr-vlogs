Welcome to AJR Vlogs!

AJR Vlogs is a personal vlog website created on September 1, 2020 as a lab exercise for a webpage designing course. Additionally, its creation is also intended to showcase the author’s skills in HTML, CSS, JS, Jquery, and other front-end and back-end coding in web designing.

What is the purpose you ask? Simply, it is in order to inspire others who dream to be a successful web designer like how the author once dreamt of being one.

“Excellence is the gradual result of always striving to do better.”
-Pat Riley

Anthony Joseph Rodriguez Robles
Author

(born August 13, 1998, Manila, Philippines), is a Filipino-born Webpage Designer who developed AJR Vlogs during his college days in University of the East as the start of his love for web designing. He studied Bachelor of Science in Information Technology as his college program and Webpage Design as his major.

At first, he thought "After finishing my Information Technology program here in UE, I'll spend my days developing games as a programmer, or if that doesn't work out, I'll become an animator in big companies like Pixar, Disney, or even in Marvel movies and series!".

Despite of his initial dreams and goals, he changed his mind after a while and decided to pursue webpage development. He thought upon himself, "I think I'll go with webpage development instead. Just the thought of algorithms for games and spending my time animating or editing is exhausting enough for me. I'd be happier if I can spend my time working while also enjoy doing it. I'll give web development a shot and create a popular website instead." - Anthony thought to himself and decided to focus learning more about web designing during his first webpage course. This in turn became the beginning of his passion in designing webpages. Although he focused on webpage designing, he still attended seminars about other areas/fields in the Information Technology field as additional knowledge for him since he believed that "Knowledge is power."
